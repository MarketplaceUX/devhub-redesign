Possible Conceptual Models

Inline editing. In this model:

  • Increase positive perception by helping the developer "see" the final Marketplace app listing as he builds it along the way.
  • Experience is organized to "funnel" developers into fewer choices.
  • Experience is organized to sustain developer engagement by mixing in emotional peaks amidst the emotional valleys and by guiding the developer through the process.
  • See the user flow »
  • See the prototype »

Q&A. In this model:

  • Increase positive perception by explicitly reducing steps through up-front Q&A. Q&A produces a "personalized" set of forms that the developer can complete to create his/her app.
  • This is a heavily guided experience. Through Q&A, the experience anticipates what the user needs next. The experience also uses previous answers/choices to drive further Q&A that guides user to best/most logical answer or choice.
  • Experience is organized to "funnel" developers into fewer choices and to sustain engagement/interest as above.
  • See the user flow »

Better forms. In this model:

  • Increase positive percepton by chunking forms into smaller pieces, lightening the cognitive load for each page of the experience.
  • Developers get in/out quickly because we provide standard UI widgets/controls they already know and don't need to think about. But these are also good looking, provide modern white space, and are accompanied by the kind of "input help" that assists developers in making good choices the first time around.
  • Experience is organized to "funnel" developers into fewer choices and to sustain engagement/interest as above.
  • See the user flow »
