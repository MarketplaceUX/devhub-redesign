Audience

The primary DevHub audience consists of these users:

  1. Developers with existing Marketplace apps
  2. Developers who are new to Marketplace apps

Additionally DevHub should support the following additional users. "Support" here means that we will direct these users to the right sources of information (vs creating that content within Debvhub).

  1. Potential partners looking for information about Marketplace and/or about partnering with Mozilla
  2. General public looking for information about Marketplace

Things We Know

The following is an evolving list of things we know from our research that we are using to inform our design strategy.

  1. The app submission process for Marketplace (and others) is tedious and often confusing for developers.
  2. Developers want to understand submission requirements before they get involved in the submission process.
  3. Developers want better support around requirements, eg a checklist of steps to complete or a checklist of screenshot sizes.

Assumptions & Hypotheses

The following is an evolving list of assumptions and hypotheses that we are using to inform our design strategy.

  1. Marketplace is one of several app distribution channels developers use. Hypothesis: Currently we can't compete on prestige or profit. But we can compete on the ease-of-use and transparency of the submission workflow and interface.
  2. Developers who use Marketplace as a distribution channel do so because their app is first and foremost a Firefox OS or Firefox app. Hypothesis: Marketplace developers will not submit Android-only apps. Please discuss in this Github issue.
