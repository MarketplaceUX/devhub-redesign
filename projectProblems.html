App Submission

  • Create Manifest
    Many developers come to submit their web app but have but dont have a manifest file, the submit manifest section could have a link to create a manifest, this would be helpful for developers who dont have manifest yet
  • Privacy Policy
    A privacy policy template could be shown for developers who are not sure what to include in the Privacy Policy Section
  • Content Rating
    A shorter method for content rating if possible
  • Analytics
    An analytics section once the app is published like the number of downloads, download for every country, etc.
  • Publish a new version on the app
    An easy method to update the version of an already published app
